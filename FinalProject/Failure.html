FAILURE

Simple and straight to the point.

Failure made it's own page because it's the most important lesson. Failure itself is the lesson. That's all there is to it. Not a step back, but a step forward to a better direction because you learned what not to do.

Don't ever take this lesson for granted, and don't ever take yourself for granted either.
